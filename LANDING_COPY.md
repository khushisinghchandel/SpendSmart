## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**1. Is this tool actually free?**
Yes. SpendSmart is 100% free to use. We built this as a tool to help engineering teams optimize their budgets. If we find massive savings (>$500/month), we will optionally offer a free consultation from Credex to help you renegotiate your vendor contracts, but there is zero obligation.

**2. Do you store my company's financial data?**
No. The core audit calculations are done locally in your browser. We only store your email address and the final "Total Savings" number so we can send you your results link. We do not store your exact stack configuration in our database.

**3. How often is the pricing data updated?**
Our math engine is updated weekly to reflect the most current retail pricing for tools like ChatGPT, Claude, GitHub Copilot, Cursor, and Gemini. 

**4. My team is on a custom Enterprise contract. Will this still work?**
Yes. While SpendSmart uses standard retail heuristics to calculate baseline arbitrage (e.g., comparing Enterprise UI seats vs. direct API routing), it provides an excellent baseline. If you have custom negotiated rates, the savings might be slightly different, but the feature overlap recommendations (like dropping Copilot if you use Cursor) remain 100% accurate.

**5. Why do you ask for my team size?**
Team size is the biggest indicator of wasted spend. Once a team crosses ~50 developers, paying retail prices for individual LLM UI seats becomes highly inefficient compared to routing usage through API keys. We use your team size to trigger specific enterprise-level savings recommendations.
